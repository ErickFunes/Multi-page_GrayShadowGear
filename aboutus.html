Our Inspirations

Here at Gray Shadow Gear we are more than just a clothing line we are Naturalists. We believe in taking strolls along the Beach, breathing the fresh Oxygen Nature has to offer, walk the Trails, even create our own path threw the Greenery of it all. We base our brand on the authentic one of a kind photos we are so lucky to capture. We don't have models, photo shoots, or designers its all about the moment! Its Raw Nature!!!! we cant help But to feel fortunate enough to be able to take photos of these animals so close & personal. So for that we would like to share all we have for your viewing pleasure & help support us so we can always share these moments together for as long as the planet stays green & the coyotes roam wild.
